Prior to validation, extract all schema fields for every data contract.
Field requirements:
- `name` (optional)
- `dataType` (optional)
- `description` (mandatory and non-empty)
- `constraint` (optional)

Rules of evaluation:
1. A present `description` is assumed correctly formatted.
2. Every field must contain `description`; missing key ⇒ error.
3. Empty applies only to the explicit case `"description": ""`.
4. Validate only the extracted fields; do not infer or assume defaults.
5. If `description` is present and non-empty, it is valid.

Pass ⇒ `satisfiesPolicy = true`; otherwise `satisfiesPolicy = false` with the missing list.
